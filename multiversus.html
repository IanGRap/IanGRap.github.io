Tools: Unreal

Duration: October 2019 - Currently

Team Size: ~60

Roles: Game Designer

I am a game designer working at Player First Games on the free to play platform fighter Multiversus, which just launched in May 2024. As a designer, I own the designs for fighters, emphasizing clarity, authenticity, novelty, cooperative play and engaging loops. In particular, I am the lead designer for Samurai Jack, Agent Smith from The Matrix, Marvin the Martian and Taz from Looney Tunes, Garnet from Steven Universe, as well as unannounced upcoming fighters. Some of my primary responsibilities include creating pitch documents for fighters, collaborating daily with animators, implementing frame data on fighter attack animations, writing and debugging mechanics through Unreal blueprints for online play and participating in daily playtests. I also led the redesign of our perks system, helped script content for the PVE Rifts mode and even participated in press interviews in the build up to our release and character launches.
